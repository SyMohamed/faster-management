Quickstart Guide

Everything you need to get started with FASTER Lab workspace

Welcome to FASTER Lab

Your centralized workspace for orders, chemicals, assets, facility planning, safety, and project management. This guide walks you through everything.

1 Getting Started
1

Sign in to the workspace

On the login page, enter your username and password. If you're a new member, click Request Access and fill out the form — an admin will approve your account.

2

Explore the dashboard

After signing in you'll see the workspace dashboard with quick stats, tool cards, and recent activity. Click any tool card to jump in.

3

Use the global search

Press Ctrl + K (or Cmd + K on Mac) from the dashboard to search across all tools — orders, chemicals, assets, bookings, and safety reports.

4

Switch themes

Click the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner to toggle between dark and light mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

3 Roles & Permissions

Admin

Full access
  • Approve/reject new members
  • Manage all orders, assets & chemicals
  • Approve facility bookings
  • Nominate project managers
  • Mark orders as delivered
  • View pending requests panel

Member

Read & write
  • Add & edit orders
  • Manage chemicals & assets
  • Request facility bookings
  • Submit safety reports
  • Raise order requests
  • Work on project tasks

Viewer (Guest)

Read only
  • Browse all data
  • Search across tools
  • View dashboards & charts
  • Cannot add, edit, or delete
  • Orange banner shown at bottom
  • Sign in as member to write
4 Order Request Flow
1

Raise a request

Go to Orders Tracker and click + Raise Request. Describe what you need and nominate a team member to handle the procurement.

2

Admin reviews

The admin sees all pending requests in the Order Requests from Team panel with date, details, and nominee. They can approve or manage each request.

3

Track progress

Your personal dashboard shows orders in three tabs: Requested, Approved, and Done. Click View All Orders for the full tracker.

5 Tips & Shortcuts
Keyboard shortcut Press Ctrl + K (or Cmd + K) on the dashboard to instantly search across all modules.
Real-time sync All pages sync live with Firebase. When a teammate adds or edits anything, you see it instantly — no need to refresh.
CSV export On the Orders page, click Export CSV to download all orders as a spreadsheet. Great for monthly reports.
Notifications The bell icon on the dashboard shows pending approvals (admin), booking results (members), and open safety reports.
6 FAQ & Troubleshooting
How do I get access?▶
Click New Member? on the login page and fill out the request form. An admin will review and approve your account. You'll be able to sign in once approved.
I see "View-only mode" — what does that mean?▶
You're signed in as a guest/viewer. This lets you browse all data but not make changes. Ask an admin to upgrade your account to member role, or sign in with a member account.
The page shows "Offline" — is data lost?▶
No data is lost. The app connects to Firebase in real-time. If you see "Offline", check your internet connection. Once reconnected, everything syncs automatically.
How do I become a project manager?▶
An admin nominates project managers from the Project Management page. Once nominated, you can create projects, assign teams, and manage Kanban task boards.
How do facility bookings work?▶
Go to Lab Facility Planning, select a facility (LPST, HPST, or RCM), pick your dates, and submit a booking request. An admin reviews and approves it. You'll be notified of the result.
Can I use this on my phone?▶
Yes — all pages are fully responsive. Open the same URL in your mobile browser. You can also add it to your home screen for quick access.
How do I report a safety issue?▶
Go to Safety Hub and click Report Issue. Add a title, description, severity, and optional photo. You earn +1 point for reporting and +2 points for fixing an issue.
